Since 2017, we've orchestrated a series of successful summer camps, the number of which has grown beyond nine, spanning various locations throughout Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. This holistic program goes beyond just coding skills. We provide a platform that fosters a love for learning, encourages creativity, and equips campers with the problem-solving skills needed to thrive in the digital age. In addition to year-round programs, our summer coding camps provide parents with a productive option for their kids to spend their summers while enhancing their overall development.
